# MOT Dictionary

Maps MOT advisory/failure phrases to risk signals. This is the product moat — it compounds with real-world usage.

Format: phrase pattern | category | severity (1-10) | typical cost band | notes

## Structure (to be populated)

Categories: brakes, suspension, steering, engine, exhaust, tyres, lights, bodywork, other

Severity scale:

- 1-3: Minor (cosmetic, wear items within tolerance)
- 4-6: Moderate (attention needed soon, affects safety margin)
- 7-9: Serious (safety concern, likely MOT failure if not addressed)
- 10: Critical (immediate safety risk, vehicle should not be driven)
